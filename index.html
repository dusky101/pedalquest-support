Quick help

The trail isn’t drawing on the map

Open Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Pedal Quest and make sure it’s set to “While Using the App”. The trail starts drawing once a few GPS points have been captured — an open space with a clear view of the sky works best for the first fix.

My phone locked mid-ride — did it still track?

Yes. Pedal Quest keeps recording while the screen is locked, so a phone in a backpack still captures the trail, distance and speed. iOS shows a small blue indicator in the status bar while that’s happening, and tracking stops as soon as the ride ends.

How do I turn off the weather feature?

In the app: Settings → Grown-up only → Local weather and toggle it off. When off, no weather requests are made and the weather cards disappear from the home and paused screens.

How do I delete every ride?

In the app: Settings → Grown-up only, then hold the “Delete every ride” button for 1.5 seconds. The deletion syncs across every device signed in to the same iCloud account.

I’ve forgotten the grown-up PIN

The PIN is stored in the iOS Keychain and can’t be recovered remotely. If you can’t remember it, deleting and reinstalling the app will clear the PIN — your iCloud-synced rides and badges return automatically when you sign back in.

How do I share ride photos with the rest of the family?

In the app: Settings → Me → App look and feel → Ride photos → Save manually (or Save automatically). A small save button then appears on each ride photo so you can move it into your Photos app and share it from there.
